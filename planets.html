Inner Planets

This is the planet Mercury! It is the closest planet to the sun while also being the fastest and smallest planet in the solar system Some other facts are:

  • 1. It's rotated by 2 degrees, making it almost upright at all times
  • 2. Because it's almost upright, it doesn't experience any seasons
  • 3. Its surface is similar to our moon

This is the planet Venus! It's the second closet planet and the hottest planet in our solar system. Some other facts are:

  • 1. It spins slowly spins in the opposite direction from most planets
  • 2. Is dubbed "Earth's evil twin" for its similar structure and size of Earth
  • 3. One of the few planets that doesn't have a moon

This is the planet Earth, our home sweet home! It's, as far as we know, the only planet with life in it. Some other facts are:

  • 1. Holds over 8.2 billion humans
  • 2. Within the "Goldilocks zone" from the Sun, where temperatures make it suitable for liquid water to exist
  • 3. Its moon is slowly drifitng away, which isn't much of a worry since it doesn't move that fast

This is the planet Mars! We've explored this planet far more than the rest, since it's the only planet that won't melt our rovers and is nearby. It used to be a much warmer and wetter planet, but now it's nothing but a cold desert. Some other facts are:

  • 1. Is about half the size of Earth
  • 2. Its day is very close to ours (24.6 hrs compared to Earth's 23.9 hrs)
  • 3. Has 95 moons offically

Outer Planets

This is the planet Jupiter! The largest planet in out solar system, able to fit 1,000 Earths. some other facts are:

  • 1. It's the oldest planet in the solar system, being from the Sun's formation 4.6 billion years ago
  • 2. The Great Red Spot is actually a storm bigger than Earth
  • 3. Though itself most likely doesn't support life, Europa, one of Jupiter's moons, could due to a ocean existing on that moon

This is the planet Saturn! Though its rings are the most visible across the solar system, it's actually not the only planet with rings. Some other facts are:

  • 1. It's the second largest planet, mostly made of helium and hydrogen
  • 2. Its rings are made of billions of ice rocks coated in dust and other things, thought to be pieces of asteroids, moons, and planets
  • 3. The rings extend to 175,000 miles long, yet are 30 feet in vertical height

This is the planet Uranus! It's rotated 90 degrees/perpendicular from its plane of orbit, making it a very unique planet. Some other facts are:

  • 1. It was dicovered in 1781 by William Herschel, making it the first planet discovered with a telescope
  • 2. Uranus actually has faint rings that come from the collison of moons that once orbited Uranus
  • 3. Its day is 17 hours, yet its year is about 84 Earth years

This is the planet Neptune! The very last planet in our solar system and therefore, the coldest. Some other facts are:

  • 1. Was discovered in 1846 via using mathematical predictions.
  • 2. 4 times wider than Earth
  • 3. ~2.8 billion miles away from the Sun
